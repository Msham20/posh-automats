About The Company

Built around manufacturing improvement, not just machine supply.

Posh Automats was started in the year 2012 with a commitment to reduce and improve human effort in automotive and non-automotive manufacturing environments.

Company story

Our commitment to our vision and customer satisfaction encouraged continuous capability expansion. Today, Posh Automats operates with a high level of confidence and an enthusiastic team to provide complete automation and manufacturing solutions for lean production and greater productivity.

Experience and expertise are used by customers to achieve manufacturing excellence and compete globally.
