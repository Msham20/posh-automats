About Posh Automats

Engineering solutions designed around manufacturing realities.

Posh Automats was started in 2012 with a commitment to reduce and improve human effort in manufacturing environments. Over the years, continued customer trust and alignment with our vision helped expand our capabilities into complete automation and manufacturing solutions.

Today, the team supports lean production, better productivity, and stronger process confidence through practical automation systems tailored to customer requirements.
